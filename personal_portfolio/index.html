About Project

This project aims to solve the problems of traditional warehouse inventory management, such as manual stock counting, misplaced items, inaccurate records, and delayed updates. In large warehouses, these issues increase operational costs and reduce efficiency. Our system uses modern technologies like IoT devices, RFID or barcode identification, and a centralized database to monitor inventory in real time. Each product in the warehouse is attached with an RFID tag or barcode. When an item enters, moves within, or exits the warehouse, RFID readers or scanners automatically capture the data.
